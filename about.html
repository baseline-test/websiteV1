About Baseline

Baseline started with a frustration we think a lot of people share — that the energy you reach for in the afternoon tends to take more than it gives. The lift is real, but so is the dip that follows, and somewhere in between you lose the thread of whatever you were doing.

So we went looking for something steadier. Not a bigger jolt — a flatter line. We built Baseline around L-theanine, the calm-focus compound found in green tea, and left the caffeine out entirely. The result is a bar that's meant to keep you level — present, unhurried, and still yourself by the end of the day.

We're a small team, we make our bars in small batches, and we're still figuring plenty of it out as we go. If that sounds like your kind of thing — join the waitlist and grow with us.
