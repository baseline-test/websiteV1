The difference

Why there's no crash

Here's the thing — caffeine works by blocking adenosine, the molecule that slowly tells your brain it's tired. It feels great, right up until it wears off and all of that catches up at once. That's the crash. Baseline takes a different path entirely — there's no caffeine, so there's nothing to pay back later. Just L-theanine, L-tyrosine, and rhodiola, working together for a calm, steady kind of focus that doesn't drop out from under you.
